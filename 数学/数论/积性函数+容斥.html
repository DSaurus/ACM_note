积性函数+容斥

2019ICPC南昌邀请赛网络赛 G.tsy's number

题意

求∑i=1n∑j=1n∑k=1nϕ(i)ϕ(j2)ϕ(k3)ϕ(i)ϕ(j)ϕ(k)ϕ(gcd(i,j,k))\sum_{i=1}^n\sum_{j=1}^n\sum_{k=1}^n\frac{\phi(i)\phi(j^2)\phi(k^3)}{\phi(i)\phi(j)\phi(k)}\phi(gcd(i,j,k))∑​i=1​n​​∑​j=1​n​​∑​k=1​n​​​ϕ(i)ϕ(j)ϕ(k)​​ϕ(i)ϕ(j​2​​)ϕ(k​3​​)​​ϕ(gcd(i,j,k)) 共T组数据，T≤10000T\leq 10000T≤10000，1≤n≤1071\leq n \leq 10^71≤n≤10​7​​

题解

枚举gcd(i,j,k) = d，然后容斥一下

ans=∑d=1nϕ(d)∑i=1[nd]∑j=1[nd]∑k=1[nd][gcd(i,j,k)==1]ϕ(i∗d)ϕ((j∗d)2)ϕ((k∗d)3)ϕ(i∗d)ϕ(j∗d)ϕ(k∗d)=∑d=1nϕ(d)∑s=1[nd]μ(s)∑i=1[nd∗s]∑j=1[nd∗s]∑k=1[nd∗s]ϕ(i∗d∗s)ϕ((j∗d∗s)2)ϕ((k∗d∗s)3)ϕ(i∗d∗s)ϕ(j∗d∗s)ϕ(k∗d∗s)=∑d=1nϕ(d)∑s=1[nd]μ(s)∑i=1[nd∗s]ϕ(i∗d∗s)ϕ(i∗d∗s)∑j=1[nd∗s]ϕ((j∗d∗s)2))ϕ(j∗d∗s)∑k=1[nd∗s]ϕ((k∗d∗s)3)ϕ(k∗d∗s) \begin{aligned} ans &= \sum_{d=1}^n\phi(d)\sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\sum_{j=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\sum_{k=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]} [gcd(i,j,k)==1]\frac{\phi(i*d)\phi((j*d)^2)\phi((k*d)^3)}{\phi(i*d)\phi(j*d)\phi(k*d)}\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^n\phi(d)\sum_{s=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\mu(s)\sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}\sum_{j=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}\sum_{k=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]} \frac{\phi(i*d*s)\phi((j*d*s)^2)\phi((k*d*s)^3)}{\phi(i*d*s)\phi(j*d*s)\phi(k*d*s)}\\ &= \sum_{d=1}^n\phi(d)\sum_{s=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\mu(s)\sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}\frac{\phi(i*d*s)}{\phi(i*d*s)}\sum_{j=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}\frac{\phi((j*d*s)^2))}{\phi(j*d*s)} \sum_{k=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}\frac{\phi((k*d*s)^3)}{\phi(k*d*s)}\\ \end{aligned} ​ans​​​​​​=∑​d=1​n​​ϕ(d)∑​i=1​[​d​​n​​]​​∑​j=1​[​d​​n​​]​​∑​k=1​[​d​​n​​]​​[gcd(i,j,k)==1]​ϕ(i∗d)ϕ(j∗d)ϕ(k∗d)​​ϕ(i∗d)ϕ((j∗d)​2​​)ϕ((k∗d)​3​​)​​​=∑​d=1​n​​ϕ(d)∑​s=1​[​d​​n​​]​​μ(s)∑​i=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​∑​j=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​∑​k=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​​ϕ(i∗d∗s)ϕ(j∗d∗s)ϕ(k∗d∗s)​​ϕ(i∗d∗s)ϕ((j∗d∗s)​2​​)ϕ((k∗d∗s)​3​​)​​​=∑​d=1​n​​ϕ(d)∑​s=1​[​d​​n​​]​​μ(s)∑​i=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​​ϕ(i∗d∗s)​​ϕ(i∗d∗s)​​∑​j=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​​ϕ(j∗d∗s)​​ϕ((j∗d∗s)​2​​))​​∑​k=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​​ϕ(k∗d∗s)​​ϕ((k∗d∗s)​3​​)​​​​

可以归纳证明，

∑i=1[nd]ϕ((i∗d)k)ϕ(i∗d)=d(k−1)∗∑i=1[nd]i(k−1) \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\frac{\phi((i*d)^k)}{\phi(i*d)} = d^{(k-1)}* \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}i^{(k-1)} ∑​i=1​[​d​​n​​]​​​ϕ(i∗d)​​ϕ((i∗d)​k​​)​​=d​(k−1)​​∗∑​i=1​[​d​​n​​]​​i​(k−1)​​

所以

ans=∑d=1nϕ(d)∑s=1[nd]μ(s)∗(d∗s)3∗[nd∗s]∗∑i=1[nd∗s]i∗∑i=1[nd∗s]i2 \begin{aligned} ans &= \sum_{d=1}^n\phi(d)\sum_{s=1}^{[\frac{n}{d}]}\mu(s)* (d*s)^3*[\frac{n}{d*s}]* \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}i* \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{d*s}]}i^2 \end{aligned} ​ans​​​=∑​d=1​n​​ϕ(d)∑​s=1​[​d​​n​​]​​μ(s)∗(d∗s)​3​​∗[​d∗s​​n​​]∗∑​i=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​i∗∑​i=1​[​d∗s​​n​​]​​i​2​​​​

设T=s∗dT=s*dT=s∗d,则

ans=∑T=1n(T3∗[nT]∗∑i=1[nT]i∗∑i=1[nT]i2)∗∑d∣Tϕ(d)∗μ(T/d) \begin{aligned} ans &= \sum_{T=1}^n (T^3* [\frac{n}{T}]* \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{T}]}i* \sum_{i=1}^{[\frac{n}{T}]}i^2)* \sum_{d|T}\phi(d)* \mu(T/d) \end{aligned} ​ans​​​=∑​T=1​n​​(T​3​​∗[​T​​n​​]∗∑​i=1​[​T​​n​​]​​i∗∑​i=1​[​T​​n​​]​​i​2​​)∗∑​d∣T​​ϕ(d)∗μ(T/d)​​

前面一部分和它的前缀和显然可以预处理，后面是两个积性函数的狄利克雷卷积，设为f(T)，p为与T互质的质数，则有

f(pk∗T)=∑i=0k∑d∣Tϕ(pi∗d)∗μ(pk−i∗Td)=(ϕ(pk)−ϕ(pk−1))∗f(T) \begin{aligned} f(p^k*T) &= \sum_{i=0}^k\sum_{d|T}\phi(p^i*d)* \mu(\frac{p^{k-i}* T}{d})\\ &= (\phi(p^k)-\phi(p^{k-1}))* f(T) \end{aligned} ​f(p​k​​∗T)​​​​=∑​i=0​k​​∑​d∣T​​ϕ(p​i​​∗d)∗μ(​d​​p​k−i​​∗T​​)​=(ϕ(p​k​​)−ϕ(p​k−1​​))∗f(T)​​

可以用线性筛预处理，然后就是n\sqrt n√​n​​​的套路了，总复杂度为O(n+T∗n)O(n+T*\sqrt n)O(n+T∗√​n​​​).
